Airtable - Equipment Borrowing and Returning System
Airtable - ระบบยืมคืนอุปกรณ์

Since MobiLib has a lot of electronic devices and can lend them to members of the organization to use in various activities , I have designed a MobiLib office equipment lending system using the Airtable tool, which is simply a Spreadsheet + Database + Automation. In addition, the mobile operating system can download the Airtable app from Play store or App store to use. Therefore, I chose this tool to create a equipment lending system.

I managed everything from creating a database and managing the equipment list, a transaction system for approving the lending, a form for borrowing equipment, to an automatic email sending system to notify the Chief Information Officer and notify the borrower when the loan is approved/disapproved.
